Key Focus Areas:

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is transforming the way brands measure their influence in the world of AI-powered search. Instead of focusing on traditional SEO, GEO shifts the attention to how generative search engines and LLMs (Large Language Models) like Perplexity or ChatGPT cite and reference your domain or content in their answers.

Recently, I built a scraping MVP that connects a Puppeteer-based crawler with a modern web dashboard. The workflow is simple: submit a query to an LLM engine, extract all cited sources, analyze how often your target domain appears, and push the structured results—including key GEO KPIs—into a user-friendly dashboard.


Why does this matter?

With GEO, you can measure metrics like:

Target Domain Citations

How often does the LLM cite your site?

Search Position

Where does your domain appear among all citations?

Citation Rate & Strength

What share of total citations do you own, and how far ahead or behind are you compared to competitors?

Citation Diversity

ow many unique domains does the LLM use?

These KPIs allow marketers and content strategists to:

  • Track the real impact of their content in the new generative search ecosystem
  • Spot opportunities
  • Adapt campaigns to influence LLMs as the next search frontier

This MVP is a step toward continuous, data-driven GEO monitoring—making LLMs work for your brand, not just traditional search engines.
