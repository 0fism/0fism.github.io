Data analysis and visualization

Dual combination

The chart is combined the line chart and bar chart to display the correlation and trend of measure of Profit and sale quantity number from 2018 to 2021. The chart showed a trend of decreasing profit and quantity sales from Q1 2018 to Q1 2021.

Packed Bubbles

Grouping the insight from visualization 1 and internal reports from the region manager, we are going to drill the question on the higher return rate. Plus, we must exclude the most profitable countries if these countries have good sales result in KPI. According to the graph, the majority of profitable countries are United Stated (23.5%), China (9.5%) and the third is France (7.8%). Other countries with profit margins greater than 5% are also included in the same group. They are United Kingdom, Germany, Australia, and Mexico. Our research should be focused on “Include other” countries that represent less profitable countries.

OpenStreetMap

The darker red color indicated the higher return rate count by the total purchase order. The charts showed the highest number of return goods are in Australia(14.2%), Mexico(13.7%), and China (9%). Combine with Packed Bubbles, these countries provided an insight, the higher quantity of return goods still performance a good result in profit KPI in the overall country population.

Horizontal Bars

Define the low-profit counties with a high amount of returned goods order using the horizontal bar chart. The blue represented the positive total profit balance, whereas the orange colors showed the negative profit balance. The chart displayed the majority of issue countries which simultaneously fit the result of Chart 3 and Chart 4. If the business continues to do in this way, Turkey (-32.5%) and Nigeria (-24.6%) continue to sell items with a lower profit margin, they keep losing up to 25–30% which may result in an ongoing financial problem.

Stacked Bars

Specific research on Turkey (orange in color) and Nigeria (blue in color), sub-categories are divided into two dimensions through the stacked bards. One is standard order without returns, and the other is with returns. To a larger extent, with returned rate is much less profit than without returned goods. Phones suffered the greatest loss in the sub-Category in the same period, followed by machines and copiers.

Treemaps

The treemaps are shown the nested rectangles of which returned goods reasons is the most impact in two countries. The top 3 least profit sub-categories were researched on their returned reasons. The three reasons of “different from what was ordered” and “unwanted item” and “defective” are similar in scale which is over 25%. They occupied over 50% of all returned reasons.
